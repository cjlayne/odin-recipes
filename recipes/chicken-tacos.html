Grilled Chicken Tacos

Description

This delicious grilled chicken is perfect for tacos, burritos, bowls or quesadillas. This chicken can be used so many different ways and is always delicious. The chicken can even be served simply with rice or salad. The recipe is super simple and just requires a little preparation and you will have some amazing chicken perfect any way you want to serve it.

Ingredients

  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 1 Tbsp adobo sauce from a can of chipotle chiles
  • 1 Tbsp sweet paprika
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs, halved lengthwise to make 4 thin chicken cutlets
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • Tortillas
  • Any taco toppings you would like!

Steps

Marinating The Chicken

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the lime juice, adobo sauce, paprika, cumin, and oregano.
  2. Season the chicken with the salt and pepper, and place the chicken in the marinade.
  3. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and let marinate in the refrigerator for at least an hour ot overnight

Grilling The Chicken

  1. Remove the chicken from the marinade, letting the excess drip back into the bowl. Place chicken on a platter or plate and let sit at room temperature for about 30 minutes.
  2. Heat a gas or charcoal grill to medium-high heat.
  3. Add the chicken to the grill and cook until it is nicely charred, about 5 minutes. Turn the chicken over and cook until the other side has grill grill marks, about 5 minutes, and the chicken is cooked through.
  4. Remove chicken from the grill and cut into 1/2-inch cubes
  5. Warm tortillas on stove top or in microwave, add chicken to tortillas and top with any toppings you prefer.
