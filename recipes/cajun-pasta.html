Cajun Pasta

Description

Homade Cajun Pasta is a delicous and simple recipe, perfect for weeknight dinners. Seasoned chicken breast tossed in an amazing creamy cajun sauce over fettuccine noodles, it is sure to become a family favorite. The best part is that it takes 30 minutes or less, making it perfect for busy weeknights.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 2 bonless skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 2 Tbsp unsalted butter
  • 2 Tbsp cajun seasoning, divided
  • 3 garlic cloves miced
  • 2/3 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesean cheese
  • 2 Tbsp parsley, finely chopped to serve

Steps

  1. Bring a large part of water to a boil and add 1 Tbsp of salt. Cook pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain in strainer.
  2. Filet chicken breasts, Then season all over using 1 1/2 Tbsp of cajun seasoning.
  3. In a large skillet heat 2 Tbsp oil ove medium heat. Once hot, add the chicken and sear on both sides. Sear until cooked through and measures 165 on a meat thermometer.
  4. Transfer cooked chicken to cutting board and slice into thin strips. Cover to keep warm.
  5. In the same skillet over medium heat add butter. Once melted, add garlic and saute until fragrant. Add diced tomatoes and saute another 2-3 minutes.
  6. Add in the heavy whipping cream, remaining cajun seasoning, and parmesean cheese. Bring to a simmer stirring often. Add more seasoning to taste
  7. Add sliced chicken and cooked pasta to tge sauce and toss until combined and both pasta and chicken are warmed through.Serve with freshly grated parmesean and chopped parsley.
